# 🧠 Saarthi

### Your AI Companion for Mental Wellness

Saarthi is a mental health support platform designed to provide accessible emotional guidance, self-care resources, and meaningful conversations through an AI-powered chatbot.

Built with the vision of making mental wellness support more approachable, Saarthi creates a safe and supportive digital space where users can reflect, learn, and seek guidance whenever they need it.

🌐 Live Demo: https://reya-doshi.github.io/Saarthi/

---

## ✨ Features

- 🤖 AI-powered mental health chatbot
- 💬 Interactive and supportive conversations
- 🌱 Mental wellness resources
- 🧘 Self-care and mindfulness guidance
- 📱 Responsive and user-friendly interface
- 🌐 Accessible anytime, anywhere

---

## 📖 About

Mental health support is not always easily accessible. Saarthi aims to bridge this gap by providing a digital companion that offers guidance, encouragement, and wellness-focused interactions.

The platform leverages AI to create engaging conversations while promoting awareness, self-reflection, and emotional well-being.

## 💡 Inspiration

Mental well-being is an essential part of everyday life, yet many individuals hesitate to seek support due to stigma, accessibility challenges, or lack of awareness.

Saarthi was created to serve as a compassionate digital companion that encourages users to prioritize their mental wellness and seek help when needed.

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

Saarthi is intended to provide general wellness support and informational guidance. It is not a substitute for professional medical, psychological, or psychiatric advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a qualified healthcare professional or local emergency services immediately.
